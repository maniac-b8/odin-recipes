Matts New York strip steak

This is my favorite steak to make for the family. it is sweet but still has the "steak flavor" Below I will list the ingredients to make this steak

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs new york strip steaks
  • Taryaki Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Salt
  • Peprika
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. * Pour 4tbsp of Taryaki and Soy Sauce into a mixing bowl
  2. * Add 2 tbsp of brown sugar
  3. * Add 1 tbsp of Garlic (salt / powder) / Onion Powder & Peprika
  4. * Mix well and let steak sit in for 5 minutes on each side
  5. * Cook on stove with oil until doneness of choice is reached
  6. * Enjoy
